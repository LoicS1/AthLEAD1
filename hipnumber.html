Reusable Hip Number

Sleek. Smooth. Flexible. Our resuable hip number takes cutting edge digital and material technology and fuses it with one of the most widely practiced sports of all time, all in a low-cost design.

The front of our sticker replacement features a very thin LED display that shows numbers in a manner similar to stadium lights. Any number from 0 to 99 can be produced, more than enough to accomodate any amount of runners in a single race. Since not much power is required to power this display, small rows of calculator like solar panels are directly underneath, providing enough energy to sufficiently power the LEDs. The thin plastic layer covering the LEDs is resting on an light aluminum body, chosen because of the flexibility and low cost and weight of the metal.

From left to right: Isometric, front, rear, and side views.

In case conditions are not ideal for solar power to be extracted, energy is stored in a battery directly connected to the solar panels. It is located on the bottom back of the product and the form factor is akin to a smart phone battery. Directly above is our means of attaching the product to shorts while racing. A light but durable sheet metal clip is stuck onto the back of the product, eliminating any sort of residue or non reusable methods of attachments. Lastly, on the upper left of the backside there is a spot for an NFC chip. This chip allows for contactless transfer of data and is currently in use in many forms of technology such as Apple Pay today. This chip will allow the information from the auto check in system to be wirelessly transported to the sticker, changing the number on the LED light.
