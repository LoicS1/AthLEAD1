Coaches and Meet Management

AthLEAD is officially endorsed by Coach Williams, a prominent figure in California coaching and the head cross country and track coach at Dublin High, who is largely responsible for the outstanding success both boys and girls teams have experienced.

We've also reached out to multiple coaches in the Bay Area for their input.

Additionally, AthLEAD is fully compatible with the timing software used by local companies like Diablo Timing and Adkins Trak West, enabling a streamlined process from check-in to posting of race results on Athletic.net.

A word about cost

It would seem as if the price point for the product (each hip number would be valued at around $6.50, according to our calculations given the cost of currently available materials) would be too expensive, given that a plastic hip sticker cost 100 times less, according to vsathletics. But given that two plastic hip stickers are required every race, not even counting the cost of wear on clothing, after an average season of 20 races, $2.40 is already saved. Each athlete with a reusable hip number will also save, through the app, about $1.50 in meet management costs per season (this figure accounts for the cost of the check-in I-pads that must be bought), and $0.50 in trash collection. So, after just about a season and a half, the system becomes profitable for coaches and meet managers.
